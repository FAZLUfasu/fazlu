Privacy Policy - Unix Aquatics Pvt Ltd

Data Collection

Unix Aquatics Pvt Ltd collects personal information such as your name, email address, and usage data when you use our services or interact with our content. This information helps us improve our products and services, and provide a personalized experience. As a leading provider of aquatic products and services, we prioritize the joy and benefits of aquatic activities for all our users.

Data Usage

The information we collect is used to provide, maintain, and improve our services. We use your data for purposes such as customer support, transactional purposes, and for sending important notifications related to your account. Our mission is to deliver top-quality aquatic products and services, which is why we aim to create a seamless experience for all users through our data-driven approach.

Data Sharing

We do not share or sell your personal data to third parties. However, we may disclose your data to third-party service providers that assist us in operating our services. These third parties are bound by confidentiality agreements and are not permitted to use your data for any other purposes. Unix Aquatics Pvt Ltd takes the security of your personal information seriously. We implement industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure. In line with our commitment to physical and mental well-being, we ensure that your information remains secure and confidential, allowing you to enjoy our services without concern.

User Rights

You have the right to access, update, and delete your personal information. You can also request a copy of your data, or withdraw consent for its use at any time by contacting us through our support team. We advocate for the importance of transparency and user control in the handling of personal information, ensuring that your rights are always respected.
